# SwiftVerify Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 1, 2026

## Introduction

SwiftVerify is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

## Social Security Number (SSN) Policy

**SwiftVerify does NOT store, access, or retrieve Social Security Numbers (SSNs) or any SSN-related reports.**

We want to make it absolutely clear that:

- **No SSN Storage**: We do not store Social Security Numbers in our systems or databases.
- **No SSN Access**: We do not access Social Security Numbers during the verification process.
- **No SSN Retrieval**: We do not retrieve Social Security Numbers or SSN-related reports from any third-party services or databases.
- **No SSN Processing**: Our verification services are designed to operate without requiring or processing SSNs.

## Data We Collect

We collect only the following information for identity verification purposes:

- Email address
- Driver's license information
- Name and contact information as needed for verification

## Data Protection

All data collected is:

- Encrypted during transmission and storage
- Processed according to industry security standards
- Retained only as long as necessary for verification purposes
- Protected by strict access controls

## Third-Party Services

While we may use third-party verification services, we ensure that:

- No SSNs are shared with third parties
- All third-party services comply with our privacy standards
- Data sharing is limited to what is necessary for verification

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of data collection (note: this may prevent verification)

## Compliance

SwiftVerify complies with all applicable data protection regulations, including but not limited to:

- Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA)
- Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA)
- State and federal privacy laws
