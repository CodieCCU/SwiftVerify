# SwiftVerify Security & Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction

SwiftVerify is committed to protecting the privacy and security of all users, especially vulnerable populations accessing our services through community centers, shelters, and remote locations. This policy outlines our security practices and your privacy rights.

## 2. Data Collection

### 2.1 Information We Collect
- **Identity Information**: Name, driver's license number, email address
- **Financial Information**: Income verification documents, bank statements (when uploaded)
- **Usage Data**: Login times, IP addresses, device information, actions performed
- **Location Data**: General location (city/state) for service delivery

### 2.2 Information We DO NOT Collect
- Social Security Numbers (unless explicitly required by law)
- Credit card information
- Biometric data (unless you opt-in to future features)
- Precise GPS coordinates

## 3. Data Security

### 3.1 Encryption
- **In Transit**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS 1.3 encryption
- **At Rest**: All sensitive data stored in our database is encrypted using AES-256
- **File Storage**: Uploaded documents are encrypted and stored in secure, access-controlled storage

### 3.2 Authentication & Access Control
- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)**: Required for all user accounts
- **JWT Tokens**: Secure, time-limited session tokens (24-hour expiration)
- **Role-Based Access**: Staff, users, and administrators have different permission levels
- **Password Requirements**: Minimum 8 characters with complexity requirements

### 3.3 Network Security
- **Firewall Protection**: All servers protected by enterprise-grade firewalls
- **DDoS Protection**: Built-in protection against distributed denial-of-service attacks
- **Regular Security Audits**: Quarterly penetration testing and vulnerability assessments
- **Intrusion Detection**: 24/7 monitoring for suspicious activity

## 4. Privacy Protections

### 4.1 Data Minimization
We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide verification services and comply with legal requirements.

### 4.2 Purpose Limitation
Your data is used ONLY for:
- Identity verification for housing applications
- Fraud prevention and security
- Legal compliance and auditing
- Service improvement (aggregated, anonymized data only)

### 4.3 Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Data retained for duration of housing application process
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data deleted after 90 days of inactivity (unless legally required to retain)
- **Audit Logs**: Retained for 7 years for compliance purposes
- **Uploaded Documents**: Deleted after verification is complete or upon user request

### 4.4 Data Sharing
We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:
- With your explicit consent
- With landlords/property managers for verification purposes only
- When required by law (court order, subpoena)
- With service providers under strict confidentiality agreements

## 5. Community & Shared Device Security

### 5.1 Kiosk Mode Protections
- **Session Isolation**: Each user session is completely isolated
- **Auto-Logout**: Automatic logout after 15 minutes of inactivity
- **Data Clearing**: All local data cleared when session ends
- **Privacy Screens**: Recommended for all shared devices
- **No Local Storage**: Credentials never saved on shared devices

### 5.2 Staff-Assisted Mode
- **Limited Access**: Staff can assist but cannot view sensitive fields without permission
- **Audit Trail**: All staff actions are logged with timestamps and identifiers
- **Training Required**: All staff must complete privacy and security training
- **Consent Required**: Users must explicitly consent to staff assistance

## 6. User Rights

### 6.1 Access Rights
You have the right to:
- Request a copy of all data we have about you
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
- Export your data in a machine-readable format
- Opt-out of non-essential data processing

## 7. Compliance

### 7.1 Regulatory Compliance
SwiftVerify complies with:
- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation) - EU users
- **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act) - California residents
- **FHA** (Fair Housing Act) - Non-discrimination requirements

### 7.2 Industry Standards

## 8. Breach Notification

### 8.1 Our Commitment
In the unlikely event of a data breach:
- Affected users notified within 72 hours
- Law enforcement and regulatory bodies notified as required
- Free credit monitoring offered (if financial data affected)
- Transparent communication about what happened and remediation steps

### 8.2 Reporting Security Issues
If you discover a security vulnerability:
- **Email**: security@swiftverify.com
- **Response Time**: Within 24 hours
- **Bug Bounty**: We offer rewards for responsible disclosure

## 9. Children's Privacy

SwiftVerify is intended for adults 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

## 13. Glossary

- **Encryption**: Converting data into a code to prevent unauthorized access
- **MFA**: Multi-Factor Authentication - requiring multiple forms of verification
- **JWT**: JSON Web Token - a secure method of transmitting information
- **TLS**: Transport Layer Security - encryption protocol for internet communications
